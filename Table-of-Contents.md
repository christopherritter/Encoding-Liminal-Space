## GUIDE TO THIS MANUAL

This book operates in **two complementary registers** that work together:

- **Liminal-Technical Exposition** chapters present conceptual foundations with sustained immersion in threshold space
- **Practical Facilitation** chapters provide hands-on protocols, documentation checkpoints, and experimentation prompts

You will move between registers, oscillating between immersive understanding and grounded practice. This oscillation is the pedagogy. Expect to inhabit the boundary between technical mechanism and metaphysical principle simultaneously.

**The Documentation Instrument:** Maintain a working documentation log throughout using the **Structured Dialogue Protocol (SDP)**. Every chapter includes specific moments to record observations, parameters adjusted, outcomes, surprises, and next steps. This log becomes your primary learning instrument.

## FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLES

This manual operates according to the following principles:

**1. Dual Register Integration** — Movement between immersive exposition and practical facilitation teaches threshold navigation as lived method.

**2. Threshold Vocabulary** — Language like "crystallize," "collapse," "navigate," "encode" genuinely inhabits both technical and metaphysical domains simultaneously.

**3. Structural Parallelism** — Identity between computational and consciousness operations is asserted through grammatical structure and operational precision, not comparison.

**4. Practitioner Agency** — You decide what matters, adapt protocols to your territory, document all boundary breaks as learning.

**5. Precision About Process** — Be certain about how to operate. Be exploratory about what it means.

**6. Documentation as Learning** — Your practitioner log is the primary learning instrument. All discovery is externalized and available for future iteration.

**7. Lived Practice Over Theory** — Everything points toward action, documentation, reflection, iteration.

**8. Liminal Facilitation as Method** — This manual operates at the threshold and teaches you to navigate threshold crossing as both technical practice and consciousness transformation.
